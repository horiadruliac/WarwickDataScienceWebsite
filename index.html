About Us

WDSS is a student-led society at the University of Warwick, dedicated to spreading the word of data science to students of all backgrounds, without any of the hype.

Through our talks, teaching, research, and many other ventures, we aim to help students build practical understanding and experience of data science. Our goal is to create a community of passionate individuals who can work together, combining data scientific techniques with domain knowledege, to solve the problems students care about.
